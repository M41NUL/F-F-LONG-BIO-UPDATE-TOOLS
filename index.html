Terms & Privacy
Privacy
Your access/EAT/JWT token is used only to update your Free Fire bio through Garena's servers and is never stored on any external database. Your bio text and formatting are kept locally in your browser's history (localStorage) purely so you can reload past versions — nothing is sent anywhere except during the update request itself.
Bio Editor & History
Colors, bold/italic/underline formatting, and line breaks you apply are just plain text tags processed entirely on your device. Your last 10 successfully updated bios are saved in Bio History on this device only — clearing your browser data will remove them.
